Side Lying Leg Lifting Exercise

Instuctions

  1. Start by lying on your side on an exercise mat, with your body in a straight line and your legs stacked on top of each other.
  2. Prop yourself up on your elbow or rest your head on your bottom arm for support.
  3. Engage your core muscles to stabilize your body.
  4. Keep your top leg straight and slowly lift it upward as high as you comfortably can without rotating your hips or leaning backward.
  5. Pause briefly at the top of the movement, focusing on squeezing your outer thigh and glute muscles.
  6. Slowly lower your leg back down to the starting position with control.
  7. Repeat the lifting motion for the desired number of repetitions, then switch to the other side and repeat.
  8. Keep your movements controlled and avoid swinging your leg for momentum.
  9. Perform the exercise with proper form to target and strengthen the muscles of your outer thighs, hips, and glutes.
