Lunges

Instuctions

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your hands on your hips.
  2. Take a step forward with one leg, lowering your hips until both knees are bent at about a 90-degree angle.
  3. Make sure your front knee is directly above your ankle and your back knee is just above the ground.
  4. Push back up to the starting position, using your front heel to drive your body back up.
  5. Repeat on the other side, alternating legs for each lunge.
  6. Keep your torso upright and engage your core for stability.
  7. Perform the exercise for the desired number of repetitions or time.
