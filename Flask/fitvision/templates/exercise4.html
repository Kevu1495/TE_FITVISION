Plank

Instuctions

  1. Start by getting into a push-up position, with your hands directly under your shoulders and your body forming a straight line from head to heels.
  2. Engage your core muscles by pulling your belly button towards your spine and tightening your abs.
  3. Hold this position, keeping your body straight and your hips level, avoiding any sagging or lifting.
  4. Focus on maintaining a neutral neck position by looking at a spot on the floor about a foot in front of your hands.
  5. Breathe steadily throughout the exercise, inhaling and exhaling deeply.
  6. Hold the plank for as long as you can maintain proper form, aiming for at least 20-30 seconds for beginners and gradually increasing the duration over time as you build strength.
  7. Once you're ready to finish, gently lower your knees to the ground and rest in a comfortable position.
