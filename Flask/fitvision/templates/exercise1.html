Bicep

Instuctions

  1. Stand upright with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Hold a dumbbell in each hand at your sides, palms facing forward.
  3. Curl the dumbbells upward towards your shoulders, keeping your elbows close to your body.
  4. Pause at the top of the movement, squeezing your biceps.
  5. Lower the dumbbells back to the starting position in a controlled manner.
  6. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
