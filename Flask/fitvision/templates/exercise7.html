Tricep Extension

Instuctions

  1. Hold a dumbbell with both hands overhead.
  2. Lower the dumbbell behind your head by bending your elbows.
  3. Keep your upper arms still and close to your head.
  4. Pause briefly, then extend your arms to lift the dumbbell back up.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of reps.
  6. Focus on using your triceps to move the weight.
